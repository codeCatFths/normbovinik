The Story So Far

Norm travels to Seattle on business for the weekend. He is plans some time to visit the Museum of Flight to see the Apollo 11 exhibit. Staying in hotels causes Norm stress and his stress dreams include aliens and flying saucers.

Suffering from lack of sleep and stress from the travel, Norm is seeing things from the corners of his eyes. An old college roommate, planning to surprise Norm at the museum, decides to play a prank and brings an alien mask to the exhibit.
